Patient Advocacy Program

Program Description

My First Call Buddy, provides peace of mind, resources, and tools, to help navigate the healthcare system, as we know it today. Whether you are a part of Obamacare, private insurance, HMO, Medicare, or Medicaid, everyone could use their own personal inside "buddy" to get them through the intricacies and medical land mines along the path to wellness. Our advocates are here to support you, 24/7, 365 days a year. When you need our help, My First Call Buddy will always be there, reaching out to you, every step of the way.

My First Call Buddy does not offer health insurance coverage or medical advice, but rather solution oriented enhancements to our members at the time of crisis.

Program Highlights

  • Access to your own personal health care patient advocate 24 hours a day, 7 days a week
  • Assist finding doctors and facilities within your network, all across the country
  • Provide educational information about medical tests, treatments and medications from RN’s and other support personnel
  • Arrange for second opinions
  • Assist in coordinating diagnostic testing as indicated
  • Research and locate private duty nursing services and home health aides
  • Facilitate hospice evaluation and services
  • Identify centers of excellence all throughout the country
  • Locate, schedule, and pull reviews, the top surgeons and rehab facilities for your specific injury within your network
  • Ensure transfer of necessary medical documentation and test results including x-ray films to a consulting physician’s appointment
  • Assist with locating assisted living facilities or group homes and arrange for evaluation for admission

How it works

  • Members call our toll-free number, 844-322-8339, and speak directly to one of our healthcare advocates.
  • There are no limits on the number of times the member and their family may use this benefit throughout their membership year.

Benefit Provider Background

Since 1988, First Call Nursing, Inc, has become one of the nation’s leading nursing contracting companies delivering qualified medical professionals to private hospitals, Kaiser, and the Veterans Administration. We employ over 150 nurses nationwide.

We developed My First Call Buddy to fill the void in the consumer support side of the healthcare system. My First Call Buddy assists its members with virtually any aspect of their healthcare support needs. We foster a member centered relationship, to help our clientele plan ahead, one medical issue at a time. In today's fast paced world, with social media, 60 hour work weeks, and a new drug commercial helping us diagnose ourselves, we need a buddy we can rely on, to help answer our calls, when we have time, not when they can squeeze us in to their schedule.

Enjoy freedom of alternative approaches to healthcare, access to holistic resources, educational and much more. The key to our success is building a team around you. You are central to our success, not the other way around.
