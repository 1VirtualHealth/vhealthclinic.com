Chiropractic

Program Description

UHS Chiropractic™ provides treatment and services at special rates from a wide choice of more than 3,000 participating Doctors of Chiropractic nationwide.
* Chiropractic benefit is not available in Vermont.

Program Highlights

  • Free initial consultation.
  • 50% savings on diagnostic services.
  • 50% savings on x-rays performed on-site.
  • 30% savings on treatments and most other services.
  • Unlimited access to care with no limits on the number of visits.
  • Each chiropractor’s verification requirements are carefully checked before being accepted in the network.
  • Items such as vitamins and durable goods are priced at the doctor’s discretion.
  • Verification Requirements:
    • Providers must meet minimum requirements for participation.
    • Unrestricted license to practice chiropractic.
    • Minimum $100,000/$300,000 professional liability coverage.

How it works

  • Members call the toll-free number listed on the back of the membership card for a listing of participating chiropractors in their area.
  • There are no limits on the number of times the member and their family may use this benefit throughout the membership year.

Variations and/or Exclusions

Chiropractors may exclude the following from discounts:

  • Vitamins (May be purchased through our vitamin service.)
  • Pillows
  • Braces and other hardware at the doctor’s discretion
Benefit may not be combined with insurance.
