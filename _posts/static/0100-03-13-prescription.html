Discount Prescription Card Program

Discounts APP for RX on the medicines you take every day of up to 75%

Program Description

This program provides members and their legal dependents the lowest price on prescription drugs, saving 10% to 85% on most medications at more than 60,000 pharmacies nationwide, including chains such as Walgreen’s, The Medicine Shoppe, HEB Pharmacy, Costco Pharmacy and Kinney Drugs, as well as independents. In addition, an Online Drug Price Check Utility provides members the ability to find the price of their prescriptions at participating locations in the zip code of their choice.

Program Highlights

  • 10% to 85% savings on most short-term and acute care medications.
  • Online Drug Price Check Utility provides members the ability to find the price of their prescriptions at participating locations in their zip code.
  • Over 60,000 participating locations, including independent, national and regional chain pharmacies nationwide.
  • Pharmacy discounts are Not Insurance, and are Not Intended as a Substitute for Insurance.

How it works

  1. To find a participating pharmacy, members call the Member Services toll-free number, Monday through Friday, 7 a.m. to 7 p.m. and Saturday, 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. Central Time, or check the website at www.nbrxcard.com.
  2. The member presents their membership card along with their prescriptions to the pharmacist. The pharmacy computer system will compare our contracted discount price with the pharmacy's retail price when the prescription is processed. The member pays the lower of the two prices directly to the pharmacy at the point of sale.

Variations and/or Exclusions

The discount is only available at participating pharmacies. The discount pharmacy card cannot be used in conjunction with insurance.
