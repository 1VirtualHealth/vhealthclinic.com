How It Works

Medical Consultations

Licensed physicians are available for consultations to diagnose non-emergent medical conditions. They will consolidate your information, develop a treatment plan, write prescriptions as needed, and utilize the pharmacy of your choice. Your physician can also request a variety of lab tests that can be ordered and administered in the convenience of your home.

Seventy percent of common medical conditions can be treated with a virtual medicine consultation. Listed below are just some of the medical issues that can be treated by a {{ site.name }} network physician.

  • Colds, coughs, and sinus problems
  • Allergies
  • Pink Eye (Conjunctivitis)
  • Flu symptoms
  • Bronchitis
  • Ear infections
  • Headaches
  • Stomachaches
  • Urinary tract infections
  • Musculoskeletal injuries (sprains, strains)
  • Bio-Identical Hormone Replacement Therapy (BHRT)
  • Acne
  • Rashes
  • Visits required for regular medicine refills
  • And much more!
